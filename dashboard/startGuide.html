Browse Library
Browse Library
This is the place where you can find items that people have put up in your library. If you like something, you can click on the borrow now button and if you have enough credits, a request for that item will be placed!

Notifications
Notification Center
The notification center is where you can get information about borrow requests, return requests, borrows, returns, when to return items and other information we may need to tell you.

Remaining
Credits
Credit Center
Keep track of your credits. These are used to borrow and lend around your community. Gain credits by lending and use them to borrow. You can also use them to get special discounts and coupons across our partners.
My Items
My Items
This is where you can add items to your community library and view and manage them. Click on the Add Multiple Items button to get started. You can also keep track of what items have been borrowed.
Account Settings
Account Settings
You can view all your account information from the settings app. You can change your account password, email-notification settings, etc., from here. We'll be adding more settings soon!
Help
Help Center
We have added a help center to answer all your questions about librorum. If your questions still remain unanswered, you can always contact us directly and we'll answer them for you!
